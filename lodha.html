About Lodha Sadahalli

Lodha Sadahalli is a thoughtfully planned ~70-acre residential estate in North Bengaluru, designed to seamlessly combine open green spaces with ultra-modern urban living. Inspired by nature’s shifting seasons, the development is lined with premium blooming landscapes including Tabebuia, Cassia, African Tulip, and Jasmine trees that bring color and elegant character across the estate.

Wide internal tree-lined boulevards, landscaped botanical parks, tranquil lakeside views, and neo-classical architectural elements create a calm, spacious sanctuary. Located in close proximity to the Kempegowda International Airport, Hebbal, Prestige Tech Cloud, and the upcoming Doddajala Metro Station, Lodha Sadahalli bridges top-tier privacy with the absolute fastest-growing commercial corridor in the region.
